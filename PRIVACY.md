# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how Legion Live handles user data.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
- Email address
- Account metadata
- Age verification status
- Usage data (streams, videos, interactions)
- Monetization activity

We do **not** sell personal data.

---

## 2. Age Verification
Age verification may be completed via:
- Self-declaration
- Optional ID verification (if enabled)

Age verification data is used solely for compliance and safety.

---

## 3. Content Monitoring
Content may be reviewed by:
- Automated moderation systems
- Human moderators

This is done to enforce safety and compliance policies.

---

## 4. Payments
Payment processing is handled by third-party providers (PayPal, Payoneer).
Legion Live does not store full payment credentials.

---

## 5. Data Security
Reasonable measures are taken to protect user data.
However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

---

## 6. Data Deletion
Users may request account deletion.
Certain records may be retained for legal or compliance purposes.

---

## 7. Policy Updates
This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the platform implies acceptance of changes.
